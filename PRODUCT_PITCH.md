# Product pitch (FootyBrain)

## Short pitch (10 seconds)

FootyBrain is a learning-first football database with quizzes—built to help you **recognize players, understand roles, and follow teams and nations** without needing live feeds or an account.

## One-liner

**Learn the game. Know the players. Become a real fan.**

## What we’re building (public beta)

- A clean, mobile-friendly football database (players, clubs, leagues, national teams)
- Curated collections and learning paths (“what should I learn next?”)
- Compare views for players and clubs
- Quiz mode with hint-driven recall
- World Cup 2026 as a **prep hub** (featured nations, groups orientation, legends/stars)—**no fixtures, brackets, or live scores**

## What makes it different

### Learning-first identity

Most football products are built for:

- **real-time utility** (scores, notifications, gambling adjacency), or
- **encyclopedic depth** (long articles).

FootyBrain is built for **learning outcomes**:

- you can name the players,
- understand their roles,
- and follow a league or national team without feeling lost.

### The “learning loop”

FootyBrain’s core loop is:

**Browse → Save → Learn → Quiz → Repeat**

Collections and learning paths keep the experience guided and low-friction.

## Competitive framing (clear differences)

### Sofascore / FotMob

- Great for: **live match tracking**
- FootyBrain: **study mode** (no live feed promises; focuses on retention)

### Wikipedia

- Great for: deep reference
- FootyBrain: guided exploration + practice (less reading, more remembering)

### Big football databases

- Great for: exhaustive stats
- FootyBrain: curated learning surfaces and consistent player identity (single registry)

## Ideal beta audience

- casual fans leveling up from “I watch highlights” to “I follow leagues”
- new supporters learning a club and its rivals
- World Cup fans preparing to recognize national teams and players

## Trust & scope (must stay honest)

- **Frontend-only** app; **no external soccer API** in beta
- **Local snapshot dataset** (date shown in-app)
- **No accounts**; device-only `localStorage` progress
- **No Transfermarkt hotlinking** for player photos; licensed assets only
- **Men’s football only** for this product phase (explicit)

## What success looks like (beta)

- users return to quiz and collections multiple times per week
- “I recognize more names” within one week of use
- World Cup hub becomes the default “prep” entry route
